The Game of Pig

Instructions

The Game of Pig is a simple yet strategic dice game where two players race to reach 100 points. On each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until they either roll a 1 (losing their turn) or choose to hold, adding their turn total to their score.

How to Play

  • Rolling the Dice:

    • If the die lands on 1, you lose all points gained during this turn, and it becomes the opponent's turn.
    • If the die lands on 2-6, that number is added to your turn total, and you may choose to roll again or hold.

  • Holding:

    • When you hold, your turn total is added to your overall score, and your turn ends.
    • The opponent then takes their turn.

  • The first player to reach 100 points wins.
